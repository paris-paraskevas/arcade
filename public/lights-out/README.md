# Lights Out

The classic light-flipping puzzle, in pure HTML5 Canvas + vanilla JS. Open `index.html` — no server, no build, no assets.

## Controls
- **Click** a light — flips it and its 4 orthogonal neighbours.
- **Arrow keys / WASD** — move the keyboard cursor.
- **Space** — toggle the light under the cursor.
- **R / Enter** — deal a new puzzle (and to start from the title screen).

## How it works
The board is a 5×5 grid of lights and the goal is to turn them all OFF. Each click flips a "plus" of cells (the one you click plus its up/down/left/right neighbours). Every puzzle is **guaranteed solvable** because it's generated by starting from an all-off board and applying a sequence of random valid clicks — replaying those exact clicks always returns the board to all-off, so a solution provably exists (unlike a random bit-pattern, half of which are unsolvable). Clearing a board advances the level (more scramble clicks = a tougher tangle), and your fewest-moves clear is saved to `localStorage`.

## Tweak ideas
- Change `N` for a bigger/smaller grid, or `baseScramble(level)` to ramp difficulty differently.
- Recolour the lit/unlit look via the `C` palette, or widen the `onGlow` blur for a softer bloom.
- Add a move-counter par or a "show me a hint" that highlights one cell of a solution.
